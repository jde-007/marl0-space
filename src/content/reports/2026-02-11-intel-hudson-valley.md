## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Hudson Valley, NY  
**Region:** Both sides of Hudson River, Westchester to Albany  
**Distance from NYC:** 60-120 miles (1-2.5 hours)  
**Best Seasons:** Fall (foliage), Spring (bloom), Year-round appeal

The Hudson Valley has emerged as NYC's premier weekend escape—a region where Gilded Age estates, farm-to-table restaurants, craft beverages, and outdoor recreation combine into a distinctly upstate experience. It's anti-Hamptons: pastoral rather than coastal, accessible rather than exclusive.

## Sample Itineraries

### Culinary & Culture Weekend
- **Day 1:** Arrive, Dia:Beacon, farm dinner
- **Day 2:** Storm King Art Center, winery visits, craft dinner
- **Day 3:** CIA lunch, antique shopping, departure

### Fall Foliage Week
- Hiking: Harriman State Park, Bear Mountain, Breakneck Ridge
- Scenic drives along Route 9 and 9W
- Apple picking, pumpkin patches, cider mills
- Peak color: Mid-October

### Historic Estates Tour
- Hyde Park: FDR Home, Vanderbilt Mansion, CIA
- Sleepy Hollow: Kykuit (Rockefeller Estate), Philipsburg Manor
- Beacon/Cold Spring: Day hike + art
