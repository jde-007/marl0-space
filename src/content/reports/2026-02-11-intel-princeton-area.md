## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Princeton, New Jersey  
**Region:** Mercer County, Central New Jersey  
**Distance from NYC/Philly:** ~1 hour from each  
**Best Seasons:** Fall (campus colors), Spring (gardens), Year-round

Princeton offers a distinctive vacation rental niche—university town culture, Revolutionary War history, and proximity to both NYC and Philadelphia. Visitors come for graduations, campus visits, corporate events, and as a base for exploring the region.
