## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Big Island of Hawaii  
**Region:** Hawaiian Islands (easternmost/southernmost)  
**Area:** 4,028 sq mi (largest US island)  
**Best Seasons:** Spring and Fall (shoulder seasons avoid peak crowds)

The Big Island represents Hawaii's most diverse vacation rental market‚Äîfrom active volcanoes to snow-capped peaks, black sand beaches to coffee country. Its sheer size and variety of microclimates mean travelers can experience multiple "destinations" on a single island.

## Competitive Intelligence

**Strengths:**
- Only place in US to see active lava (potentially)
- Most diverse landscapes of any Hawaiian island
- Lower crowds than Maui/Oahu
- Better value vacation rentals
- Authentic Hawaiian culture preserved

**Weaknesses:**
- Larger driving distances required
- Some beaches less accessible
- Fewer direct mainland flights than Honolulu
- Vog (volcanic smog) can affect air quality

**Market Position:**
Big Island is the "explorer's Hawaii" ‚Äî best for travelers who want adventure, nature, and authenticity over resort convenience. It complements rather than competes with Maui (luxury beaches) and Oahu (urban/tourist infrastructure).
