## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Acadia National Park & Bar Harbor  
**Region:** Down East Maine, Mount Desert Island  
**Park Size:** 49,075 acres  
**Best Seasons:** Spring and Fall (avoid peak summer crowds)

Acadia is the only US national park in New England and the easternmost on the mainland. The combination of dramatic coastline, Cadillac Mountain (first place to see sunrise in the US for half the year), and charming Bar Harbor makes this a quintessential New England vacation destination.

## Intelligence Summary

### Key Findings

- **Wikipedia:** 4 geographic sections covering climate, history, geography, and administration
- **WikiVoyage:** 28 linked destinations including Boston, Portland, and regional towns
- **Rich History:** 10,000+ years of Wabanaki habitation; 19th century saw wealthy "rusticators"
- **Carriage Roads:** 45 miles of motor-free roads financed by John D. Rockefeller Jr.

### Core Attractions

1. **Cadillac Mountain** — 1,530 ft, highest Atlantic coast peak; sunrise watching is iconic
2. **Park Loop Road** — 27-mile scenic drive through park highlights
3. **Jordan Pond House** — Historic restaurant famous for popovers and lawn dining
4. **Bar Harbor** — Classic New England village with shops, restaurants, whale watching
5. **Acadia's Trails** — 158 miles of hiking, including famous ladder trails (Precipice, Beehive)

## Sample Itineraries

### Classic Acadia Week
- **Day 1:** Arrive Bar Harbor, explore downtown, lobster dinner
- **Day 2:** Park Loop Road drive, Thunder Hole, Sand Beach
- **Day 3:** Cadillac Mountain sunrise, Jordan Pond House lunch
- **Day 4:** Biking carriage roads, swim at Echo Lake
- **Day 5:** Whale watching cruise, Schoodic Peninsula
- **Day 6:** Challenging hike (Precipice or Beehive), shopping
- **Day 7:** Bass Harbor Lighthouse, departure
