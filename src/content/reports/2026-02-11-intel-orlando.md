## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Orlando, Florida  
**Region:** Central Florida, Orange County  
**Annual Visitors:** 75+ million (pre-pandemic)  
**Best Seasons:** Spring, Fall (avoid summer heat and holiday crowds)

Orlando is synonymous with theme parks—Walt Disney World, Universal, SeaWorld create an unmatched concentration of attractions. The vacation rental market here is massive, with entire communities (Reunion, Champions Gate) built specifically to serve multi-family groups visiting the parks.

## Sample Itineraries

### Disney Week (6-7 Days)
- **Day 1:** Arrive, pool time, Disney Springs dinner
- **Day 2:** Magic Kingdom (full day)
- **Day 3:** EPCOT (World Showcase evening)
- **Day 4:** Rest day: pool, mini golf, resort hopping
- **Day 5:** Hollywood Studios
- **Day 6:** Animal Kingdom
- **Day 7:** Flexible: return to favorite, shopping, departure

### Universal Focus (4-5 Days)
- **Day 1:** Universal Studios
- **Day 2:** Islands of Adventure (Harry Potter)
- **Day 3:** Volcano Bay water park
- **Day 4:** CityWalk, return to favorites
- **Day 5:** Kennedy Space Center day trip

### Multi-Family Mega Trip (10-14 Days)
- Split time between Disney, Universal, SeaWorld
- Rest days at vacation home pools
- LEGOLAND day (for younger kids)
- Outlet shopping
- Natural springs day trip
