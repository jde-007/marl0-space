## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Hilton Head Island, SC  
**Region:** Lowcountry, South Carolina  
**Island Size:** 12 miles long, 5 miles wide  
**Best Seasons:** Spring and Fall (summer is hot/humid but peak season)

Hilton Head represents the refined, family-friendly end of the resort island spectrum—master-planned communities, world-class golf, 12 miles of pristine beaches, and a commitment to natural preservation that keeps the island feeling uncrowded despite its popularity.

- **Wikipedia:** 5 geographic sections covering history, demographics, and development

### Core Attractions

1. **Golf** — 33 golf courses; Harbour Town Golf Links hosts PGA's RBC Heritage
2. **Beaches** — 12 miles of wide, uncrowded, family-friendly sand
3. **Biking** — 60+ miles of paved pathways throughout island
4. **Harbour Town** — Iconic lighthouse, marina, shops, restaurants
5. **Gullah Geechee Heritage** — Native Islanders' African-rooted culture preserved

## Sample Itineraries

### Family Beach Week
- **Day 1:** Arrival, beach time, bike rentals
- **Day 2:** Beach morning, Harbour Town afternoon, lighthouse climb
- **Day 3:** Dolphin cruise, mini golf, ice cream
- **Day 4:** Sea Pines Forest Preserve, beach, BBQ at rental
- **Day 5:** Day trip to Savannah (45 min)
- **Day 6:** Water sports: kayaking, paddleboarding
- **Day 7:** Final beach day, departure

### Golf Getaway (4-5 Days)
- 18 holes daily at different courses
- Harbour Town Golf Links (bucket list)
- Atlantic Dunes, Robert Trent Jones courses
- Evening: Seafood dinners, early rest
