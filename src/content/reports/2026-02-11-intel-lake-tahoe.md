## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Lake Tahoe Basin  
**Region:** Sierra Nevada, California-Nevada border  
**Elevation:** 6,225 ft (lake surface)  
**Best Seasons:** Winter (skiing) and Summer (water sports); Spring/Fall for fewer crowds

Lake Tahoe is one of America's most versatile vacation destinations‚Äîa world-class ski destination in winter that transforms into a water sports and hiking paradise in summer. The two-state geography creates interesting market dynamics: California's South Shore offers casino-free family vibes, while Nevada's North Shore and Stateline provide gaming options.

## Competitive Intelligence

**Strengths:**
- True four-season destination (rare in US)
- Proximity to Bay Area (3.5 hours) and Sacramento (1.5 hours)
- World-class skiing AND summer water sports
- Natural beauty rivals anywhere in North America
- Wide range of accommodations (budget to ultra-luxury)

**Weaknesses:**
- Winter access can be treacherous (chain requirements, closures)
- Summer weekends extremely crowded
- Vacation rental regulations tightening in some areas
- Wildfire smoke risk in late summer

**Market Position:**
Lake Tahoe is the West Coast's premiere alpine destination‚Äîcombining Colorado-quality skiing with stunning lake access that mountain-only resorts can't match. For vacation rentals, it's ideal for groups and families who want space, kitchen access, and value compared to resort hotels.
