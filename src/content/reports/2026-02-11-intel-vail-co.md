## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Vail, Colorado  
**Region:** Eagle County, Rocky Mountains  
**Elevation:** 8,150 ft (base); 11,570 ft (summit)  
**Best Seasons:** Winter (skiing) and Summer (hiking, biking)

Vail is the largest single ski mountain in the United States, featuring 5,317 skiable acres including the legendary Back Bowls. The purpose-built Tyrolean village offers a more curated experience than historic mining towns like Aspen or Telluride.

## Sample Itineraries

### Ski Epic Week
- **Days 1-2:** Front side exploration, village orientation
- **Days 3-4:** Back Bowls (Game Creek, Sun Down, China, etc.)
- **Day 5:** Blue Sky Basin or Beaver Creek day trip
- **Day 6:** Lessons, spa, or rest day
- **Day 7:** Final laps on favorites, departure

### Summer Mountain Week
- **Day 1:** Arrive, acclimate, village exploration
- **Day 2:** Epic Discovery—gondola, ziplines, alpine coaster
- **Day 3:** Hiking—Booth Falls or Gore Creek Trail
- **Day 4:** Mountain biking or rafting on Eagle River
- **Day 5:** Day trip to Glenwood Springs hot springs
- **Day 6-7:** Additional activities, departure
