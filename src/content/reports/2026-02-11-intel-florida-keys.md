## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Florida Keys archipelago  
**Region:** Monroe County, Florida  
**Geography:** 120-mile island chain from Key Largo to Key West  
**Best Seasons:** Winter/Spring (escape northern cold; avoid hurricane season)

The Florida Keys offer a unique American experience—a chain of tropical islands connected by the Overseas Highway, each key with its own personality. From world-class diving in Key Largo to the Hemingway mystique of Key West, the Keys deliver Caribbean vibes without a passport.

## Intelligence Summary

### Key Findings

- **Wikipedia:** 3 geographic sections covering geology, climate, and ecology
- **WikiVoyage:** 24 linked destinations including Miami and Caribbean connections
- **Unique Ecosystem:** Only living coral barrier reef in continental US
- **Mile Markers:** Navigation by mile marker (MM0 in Key West to MM126 in Florida City)

### Core Attractions

1. **John Pennekamp Coral Reef State Park** — First underwater state park, exceptional snorkeling/diving
2. **Seven Mile Bridge** — Iconic engineering feat, spectacular views
3. **Key West** — Duval Street, Hemingway Home, southernmost point, sunset at Mallory Square
4. **Islamorada** — "Sportfishing Capital of the World," Theater of the Sea
5. **Bahia Honda State Park** — Best beach in the Keys

## Sample Itineraries

### Keys Road Trip (5-7 Days)
- **Day 1:** Key Largo arrival, snorkel Pennekamp
- **Day 2:** Islamorada fishing or Theater of the Sea
- **Day 3:** Marathon, Turtle Hospital, Bahia Honda beach
- **Day 4:** Drive Seven Mile Bridge, arrive Key West
- **Day 5-6:** Key West exploration, Duval Street, sunset sailing
- **Day 7:** Leisurely return, stops at favorites

### Diver's Paradise (4-5 Days)
- 2-tank dives daily at different sites
- Christ of the Abyss statue dive
- Spiegel Grove shipwreck (advanced)
- Night diving
