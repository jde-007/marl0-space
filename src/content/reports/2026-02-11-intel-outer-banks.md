## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** Outer Banks (OBX)  
**Region:** Dare & Currituck Counties, NC  
**Geography:** 130-mile barrier island chain  
**Best Seasons:** Summer peak; Spring/Fall for fewer crowds

The Outer Banks represent the classic American beach vacation—wide sandy beaches, affordable family rentals, and a laid-back coastal culture. From the Wright Brothers' first flight to wild horse herds, OBX offers a unique blend of history and nature.

### Core Attractions

1. **Wright Brothers National Memorial** — Where powered flight began (1903)
2. **Jockey's Ridge State Park** — Tallest natural sand dune on East Coast
3. **Cape Hatteras Lighthouse** — Tallest brick lighthouse in America
4. **Wild Horse Tours** — Corolla's free-roaming Spanish Mustangs
5. **Roanoke Island** — Lost Colony history, Festival Park

## Sample Itineraries

### Classic OBX Family Week
- **Day 1:** Arrive, unpack, beach time
- **Day 2:** Wright Brothers Memorial, Jockey's Ridge
- **Day 3:** Full beach day, evening mini golf
- **Day 4:** Wild horse tour in Corolla
- **Day 5:** Day trip to Hatteras: lighthouse, Buxton
- **Day 6:** Water sports: kayaking, surfing lessons
- **Day 7:** Beach morning, departure

### Adventure OBX
- Kiteboarding lessons (world-class winds)
- Kayak through maritime forest
- Deep-sea fishing charter
- Hang gliding at Jockey's Ridge
