## Market Overview

**Primary Destination:** The Berkshires, MA  
**Region:** Western Massachusetts, Berkshire County  
**Distance from NYC/Boston:** ~2.5 hours from each  
**Best Seasons:** Summer (Tanglewood), Fall (foliage), Year-round appeal

The Berkshires offer a rare combination: world-class cultural institutions (Tanglewood, MASS MoCA, Jacob's Pillow) set in pastoral New England landscapes. It's the thinking person's vacation—concerts, dance, theater, museums—wrapped in rolling hills and charming villages.

## Sample Itineraries

### Cultural Immersion Weekend
- **Day 1:** MASS MoCA (plan 4+ hours), dinner in North Adams
- **Day 2:** Tanglewood concert, Lenox village, farm dinner
- **Day 3:** Norman Rockwell Museum, Great Barrington shopping

### Fall Foliage Week
- Drive Mohawk Trail (Route 2)
- Hike Mount Greylock
- Apple picking, cider mills
- Peak color: Early-to-mid October
- Covered bridge touring

### Summer Festival Circuit
- Tanglewood concerts (Fridays, Saturdays, Sundays)
- Jacob's Pillow dance performances
- Williamstown Theatre Festival
- Barrington Stage Company
